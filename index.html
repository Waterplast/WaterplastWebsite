About Us

Waterplast® is a brand name synonymous with quality, durability and reliability. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, Waterplast is committed to fulfilling the goal of watering the nation through constant innovation in plumbing pipes, irrigation and sewerage technologies.

Our Infrastructure

Our world class manufacturing facilities are located in Prayagraj and Noida district in Uttar Pradesh, with a combined capacity to produce 7000 Metric Tons per year. The units are equipped with state of the art machinery and follow a stringent quality standards to ensure zero defect and leak proof products signified by ISO 9001:2015 and ISI 4985 certifications.

Our Distribution Network

Dealers and distributors are our partners in fulfilling the dream of making water available to everyone and everywhere. With an extensive network of dealers, Waterplast® is committed to provide exceptional services to its customers by delivering high quality products.
